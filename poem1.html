Whispers at Dusk

By You

The sky melts into amber sighs,

Leaves murmur secrets as they fall,

The wind pens verses between the pines,

And silence becomes the loudest call.

Time slows with each golden breath,

Stars gather thoughts from the deep,

Night hums lullabies of graceful death,

As dreams settle down to sleep.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

This is a simple and delicious recipe for Spaghetti Aglio e Olio. It's quick to prepare and full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (sliced)
  • Chili flakes
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan (optional)

Instructions:

1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.

2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© the garlic slices until golden brown.

3. Add chili flakes and cooked spaghetti to the pan, toss everything together.

4. Garnish with parsley and Parmesan. Enjoy!
